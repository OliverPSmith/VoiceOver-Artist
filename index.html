A little bit about me...

I am a professional British female voiceover artist and have been working in the industry since 2016. My experience spans, Commercials, E-Learning content, adult and children’s narrations, corporate work, IVR, comedy and drama radio plays and everything in between. In that time, I have worked for companies including, Colgate, Corsodyl, OLA Driver, Boots and Spotify.

Since completing my training in ... Musical theatre at Guildford School of Acting in 2012, I have been working in the performing arts sector. My experience as an actor has provided me with an excellent foundation in storytelling, building characters and understanding the essence and tone to convey with your words. I take direction well and I can help you create exactly what you’re looking for to bring your script to life. I enjoy working closely with my clients, so they walk away satisfied that my voice is selling their brand as they envisioned it. If you aren’t sure what your vision is, I also love to play and will work with you to find your sound.

I was born and raised in Surrey, England meaning I naturally have that appealing, neutral British accent which is a great fit for all sorts of genres of Voiceover. My light RP sound is easily adaptable to your needs, whether you’re after a professional sound or something more casual I can hit just the right note for you. Have a little listen to my audio reels where you can hear my versatility in range from Calm and Sophisticated to Upbeat and Friendly, Serious and Empathic to Comical and Characterful.

My Home Studio

Having a Broadcast Quality Home Studio means I can turn around most voice overs up to 500 words within 24-48 hours. Have a bigger script? Contact me and we can discuss how quickly I can get it to you.

My studio is sound treated so you get a clean, professional audio.

I record on a Sennheiser MK4 Large Diaphragm Microphone with Focusrite Scarlett 2i2 Interface.

I can provide files in WAV or MP3 format.

I am a 40 minute commute to Waterloo with access to my own car, so if you’d rather work face to face I’m more than happy to come to you.

Rates

I follow the Standard British Equity Rates Guidelines. Rates vary depending on a number of factors including whether the voice over is being Broadcast and on what platforms, script length and the category the voice over falls into.Get in contact with me today and I can get a bespoke quote across to you right away.
