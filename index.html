A little bit about me...

As the saying goes, first impressions last! The voice message that greets your customers is far more than an audio recording, it is your first opportunity to impress and instil confidence in your customers that you are a professional business. I am a professional voice over artist who is passionate about providing high quality voiceover recordings. I bring a wealth of experience to each audio recording I create and my versatility and attention to detail will bring your script alive. I have worked on a diverse range of projects including explainer videos, e-learning courses, corporate videos and commercials. I have also worked extensively with audio guides, documentaries, TV narration, voicemail greetings and Interactive Voice Response Systems (IVR). Further details about the services I can provide can be found on the Services page. My consistently reliable approach to the wealth of services that I offer is reflected in the steady core of loyal customers ... that I have built up within the industry.
